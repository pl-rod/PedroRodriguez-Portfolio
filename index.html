About Me

Hello, I´m Pedro. A frontend developer, originally from Venezuela now based in Berlin, Germany.


Constantly studying, practicing and developing in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and other technologies related to web development. Currently exploring React.


A heap of experience in gastronomy as Waiter and Bartender collected over the past seven years. I always felt fascinated by web development, to create and control this small world that lives in a website or an app, even learned DreamWeaver ! Back in the days, I know right?


Two years ago I decided to pursue my dream of becoming a web developer. While working full-time at the restaurant, I enrolled myself in various courses mainly through Codecademy (among others).


I am fully committed to learn new technologies and creating clean, accessible, responsive and reusable code in order to achieve my goals to start and nourish a successful career in the tech world.
